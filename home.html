BARA

Bara (薔薇, "rose"), also known by the wasei-eigo construction "Men's Love" (ML メンズラブ, "menzu rabu"), is a Japanese technical term for a genre of art and fictional media that focuses on male same-sex love usually created by gay men for a gay audience. This genre of manga began in the 1950’s as a fetish magazine — ADONIS (1952) — featuring gay art and content, but the popularity did not arise until the 1970’s magazine, self-titled after the genre, Barazoku (薔薇族) — “the rose tribe.”
